Our Programs

Workshop/Seminar Program

Fostering open dialogue through workshops and seminars, sparking conversations about gender stereotypes, gender socialization, mental health, and concepts like "The Men Box" and "The Possibility Box." We extend our reach to both universities and the broader community, emphasizing engagement over passive presentation.

Food Parcels Program

In collaboration with SA Harvest, we provide crucial assistance to underprivileged individuals. We extend our support to university students facing financial hardship, offering food parcels to alleviate their immediate needs and community members. By working closely with our partners and supporters like Boxer Superstores, we're committed to making a tangible impact in the lives of those facing food insecurity.
